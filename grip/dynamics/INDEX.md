# Grip Dynamics

[← Grip](../INDEX.md) · [← Home](../../README.md)

Dynamics covers what happens to the hands, forearms, and clubface during the swing — the motions that the [static grip setup](../statics/INDEX.md) influences. While statics defines starting conditions, dynamics is where those conditions play out.

## Key Idea

The grip doesn't cause the dynamics — the body does. But the grip sets the **starting conditions** that make certain dynamics easier or harder. A [strong grip](../statics/strong.md) makes it easier to close the face; a [weak grip](../statics/weak.md) makes it easier to leave the face open. The dynamics section explains these cause-and-effect chains.
