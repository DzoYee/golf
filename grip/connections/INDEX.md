# Grip Connections

[← Grip](../INDEX.md) · [← Home](../../README.md)

Connections maps how grip decisions ripple beyond the hands into the rest of the swing. The grip doesn't exist in isolation — it influences (and is influenced by) the shoulders, body rotation, and overall swing mechanics.

## Key Idea

Most golfers think of grip as "just the hands." But the hands are connected to the forearms, which are connected to the upper arms, which are connected to the shoulders. Changing the grip changes the entire chain. This section makes those connections explicit.
